Reach Us

You can reach us through any of these ways. One of the unique feature is that you can also schedule your monthly pickup of scrap.

We pick from your doorstep for FREE

We will collect scrap from the place you want us to pick up from. The scrap is weighed on Digital Scale which will give the most accurate weight of your scrap compared to the manual scale which your local scrap buyer uses.

Redeem with the option of your choice

We provide you a list of options(ofcourse apart from CASH) in return of your scrap. We have options varying from Mobile top ups to Meal Vouchers to Movie Tickets.
